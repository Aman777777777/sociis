How we open

A rolling plan, EMEA first, then the world

The institution does not settle in one place. The Room travels, a different city each time, until the network covers the world. A city joins only when it has a dated Room and a screened house. Intent, not inventory.

The kind of house

Screened once, chosen for a reason

The institution does not place its name in any house. Each is traced to its owner and tested against the safety of every guest. We favour grande dames held by families or clean owners, never by the states or funds the standard exists to change. One house per city.
