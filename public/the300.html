The 300

23 July 2026 On the iconic grounds of Tomorrowland, in collaboration with Love Tomorrow

We serve humanity by changing how the world behaves.

SociisGroup exists to change how one billion people behave, for higher standards of sustainability and dignity. The 300 is where that work begins in public.

On 23 July 2026, on the iconic grounds of Tomorrowland in collaboration with Love Tomorrow, three hundred people are brought into one room. International leaders from fourteen countries, the founders, investors, scientists and cultural figures who move things. A long lunch, voices on a stage, a song heard for the first time, then the Summit, open to you long into the night.

The day is casual chic · come at ease

A glimpse of the day

The Day

From the first table to the last song

From 10:30

Arrival and welcome

You arrive and the room gathers. A drink, a welcome, the people around you. No rush, no formality.

Midday

Lunch and the round tables

A seated lunch, then round-table conversations across the room. This is where the day does its real work, people meeting people.

Afternoon

The voices

Sarah McArthur on the life of Frances Hesselbein. Hein Wagner on the stage. Dr Tessy Ojo CBE of The Diana Award. Further voices, music and entertainment.

The first time

The song

A short teaser of Let There Be Light, and then Jean Bosco Safari performs the song live, for the first time anywhere.

From 16:00

Into the Summit

The room opens into the Summit. Full access through the evening, food and drink included, music and the night ahead of you.

What a place holds

A place at The 300 is not a ticket to an event. It is a full day inside a room of no more than three hundred, then the Summit through the evening, food and drink included.

It stands in service of The Diana Award, Under One Sky and the Nelson Mandela Foundation. And it is the founding edition, the only one that will ever be first.

What a place carries is not the day. It is the three hundred you spend it beside, the causes the room serves, and the year you were in it. Anyone can walk the grounds. Almost no one is hosted inside.

This is the founding edition. There is only one that is ever first, and after it the room is smaller and by invitation. To be here now is to be on the first list, forever.
