What this is

We serve humanity by changing how the world behaves.

We shift the mind, so that the mind shifts behaviour, and behaviour, in time, shifts the world.

SociisGroup is the first behavioural institution in the world. In plain terms: we work to change how people behave, so that standards of sustainability and dignity rise. Culture moves faster than law, so we begin with the room, the people in it, and the standards they carry out into the world. The 300 is where that work begins in public.
